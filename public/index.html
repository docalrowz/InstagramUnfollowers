What it does not do

100% client-side · MIT-licensed
No remote servers The script only talks to instagram.com using your own session cookies. No third-party endpoint is contacted.
No login required You stay signed in to Instagram in your own tab. Nothing asks for your password and nothing reads it.
No data exfiltration Whitelist and timing preferences live in your browser's localStorage. Nothing leaves your device.
Source is auditable The full TypeScript source is open on GitHub. The snippet above is the minified build of that exact code.

How to run

Three steps · 30 seconds
  1. 1

    Copy the script

    Hit Copy script above. The full minified bundle is now on your clipboard.

  2. 2

    Open Instagram DevTools

    On instagram.com, open developer tools and switch to the Console tab.

  3. 3

    Paste and run

    Paste, hit Enter. The app replaces the page and starts scanning your follows.
